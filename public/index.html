About our service

We are a smart gym fitness website that allows users to input their fitness data by simply tapping the users phone onto one of our tags that have been placed onto the gym equipment.

Simple and Convenient

Our website allows users to enter their fitness easy and also allows the user to track and chart their own fitness data over time.

NFC Technology

The tags that are placed on the gym equipment are known as NFC tags which allows your phone to communicate with them if they are simply placed in close proxmity of each other. When a user taps their phone onto one of the NFC tags they are brought to the specific page of the tag that was scanned, this allows them to quickly input their fitness data and go back to their workout routine.
